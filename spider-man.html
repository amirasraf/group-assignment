SPIDER-MAN: FAR FROM HOME

Spider-Man: Far From Home is a 2019 American superhero film based on the Marvel comic character, Spider-Man. This film is co-produced by Columbia Picture and Marvel Studios, and distributed by Sony Picture Releasing. It is a sequel to the Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017) and the 23rd film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The film was directed by Jon Watts, written by Chris McKenna and Eric Sommers. The film is starred by Tom Holland as Peter Parker/Spider-Man, Samuel L. Jackson, Zendaya, Cobie Smulders and many others. In this film, Peter Parker/Spider-Man is recruited by Nick Fury and Mysterio to face the elementals while he is on a school trip to Europe.



Spider-Man: Far From Home premiere at the TCL Chinese Theatre on June 26, 2019 and was released in United States on July 2, 2019 as the last film in Phase Three of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The film received mostly positive review for its humor, action, visual effect and Tom Holland Performance. This film reaches the sales at 1.1 billion dollars worldwide and making it as a first ever Spider-Man film to pass a billion-dollar mark.
