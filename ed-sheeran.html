ED SHEERAN

Edward Cristopher Sheeran or more known as Ed Sheeran is an English singer and songwriter. He was born in 17 February 1991 at Halifax, West Yorkshire, England. Ed Sheeran began writing songs around the age of eleven. In the year of 2011, Ed Sheeran signed with Asylum Records and release his very first album which is + (pronounced “plus”) and topped the UK Album Chart. This album also contains his first hit single “The A Team”. In 2012, Ed Sheeran won Brit Awards for Best British Male Solo Artist and British Breakthrough Act.



In June 2014, Ed Sheeran releases his second studio album which is x (Pronounced “Multiply”) and topped charts around the world upon its released. It was named second-best-selling album worldwide of 2015 and also win Album of the Year at the 2015 Brit Awards in the same year. A single from this album called “Thinking Out Loud” earned him the 2016 Grammy Awards for Song of the Year and Best Pop Solo Performance.



Ed Sheeran third album ÷ (pronounced “Divide”) was released in March 2017. The first two single from the album which is “Shape of You” and “Castle on the Hill” broke records in a number of countries by debuting in the top 2 position of the chart. He is also the first artist to have two song debuts in the US and top 10 in the same week. His fourth single from album ÷, “Perfect” reached top 1 in the US, Australia and UK. Since he became the world’s best-selling artist in 2017, he was named the Global Recording Artist of the Year. His next studio album = (Pronounced “Equal”) topped the chart in the most major market in 2021.


Ed Sheeran has sold more than 150 million records worldwide and making him on of the best-selling music artist in the world. The new Ed Sheeran album can be bought at his own website
